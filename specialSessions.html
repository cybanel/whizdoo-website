Why Special Sessions?

Not tutoring help — a lesson plan of their own

General tutoring supports what's already being taught in school. Special Sessions are different — our tutors build a custom curriculum around one subject, made specifically for your child.

Custom-built lesson plans

Every session is planned around your child specifically — their level, their pace, and their goals.

One subject, full focus

No juggling five subjects in one hour. We go deep into the one thing your child actually needs help with.

Built for every kind of learner

Whether it's a student catching up in Math, a foreign learner starting English, or a local student learning Tagalog — we tailor it to them.
